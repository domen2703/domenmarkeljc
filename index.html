i don't design for the first glance. anyone can make something that gets noticed once. i design for the version of a brand that gets looked at twice — and then gets remembered without trying to be.

that means strategy, typography, image, motion and interaction stop being separate jobs. they become one decision, made once, held all the way through — so nothing about a brand feels like it was finished by a different person than the one who started it.

i'm not interested in work that looks good for a week. i'm interested in the mark someone draws from memory a year later, without meaning to.

the first glance fades.

the second look stays.
